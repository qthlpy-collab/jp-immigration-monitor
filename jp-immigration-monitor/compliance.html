Compliance & Ethics

This project is designed with a “public information monitoring” mindset. We aim to be respectful to website policies, infrastructure, and content ownership.

Rules we follow

Robots & Terms

Check robots.txt and Terms of Service; do not scrape pages that disallow automated access.

Rate Limiting

Low frequency by default, exponential backoff, caching, and conditional requests when possible.

Attribution

Store and display source URLs; show short summaries rather than copying full text.

Privacy & Safety

We do not collect personal data. If a source contains sensitive identifiers, we redact them. We also avoid publishing scraped full-text content, focusing instead on metadata and links.

Data stored: title, date, category, source name, URL, short summary (optional)
Not stored: user accounts, personal identifiers, private documents
Limitations

Official sites can change structure or remove pages at any time. Our change detection reduces downtime, but human review remains necessary for critical decisions.
